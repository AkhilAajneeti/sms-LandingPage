FAQ'S related toadmission (2026)

SMS Varanasi is an autonomous college is approved by All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE), Ministry of Education, Government of India. The Institute is affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith, (MGKVP) Varanasi for M.Com. and all UG courses and Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow for its PG (MBA & MCA) Courses.

SMS Varanasi offers Undergraduate and Postgraduate courses in areas of Management, Commerce, Computer Science and Mass Communication.

MBA, MCA, MCOM, BBA, BCA, BCOM, BCOM(Hons), BA(Hons) Mass Communication are the courses currently running at SMS Varanasi.

The campus of SMS Varanasi is a well-equipped, carbon-free green campus. The institute offers students the best possible studying environment with its computer labs, rich library, smart classrooms, and recreational amenities.

For any queries, you can reach us by phone at the numbers shown on our official website (https://smsvaranasi.com/), visit at our Varanasi campus, or online.

You may submit an Application Online at https://smsvaranasi.com/. The majority of courses include an Entrance Exam for admission, and the procedure usually includes Registration, Document Submission and GDPI (for PG Course)

Undergraduate Programs (BBA/BCA/BCOM/BCOM(Hons)/BA(H)-MC): The admission process includes an entrance exam conducted by the institute and also through CUET. Students are required to fill out an online application form and appear for the entrance exam.

Postgraduate Programs (MBA/MCA/MCOM): Admission is usually based on academic performance followed by an entrance exam (such as CUET/CAT/MAT/CMAT/XAT/SMS Online test) and GDPI. Students are required to fill out an online application form and appear for the GD&PI.

For undergraduate courses, candidates usually need to have passed 10+2 from a recognized board. Specific percentage and discipline requirements may vary depending on the course.
