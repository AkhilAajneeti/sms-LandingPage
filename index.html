About SMS Varanasi

School of Management Sciences Varanasi, popularly known as SMS Varanasi, has been a pristine institution attracting hues of both a new age B-School as well as an archaic academic house. A 1995-born institution in the holy city of Varanasi (Kashi) witnessed an auspicious time, the childhood period of the opening economy, and received a thumping welcome by virtue of being the first of its kind of self-financed institution in the region.
