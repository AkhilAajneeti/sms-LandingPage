Computer Centre Infrastructure

In the recent past the importance and use of information technology has grown tremendously in academics and research across a wide spectrum of disciplines. IT is making a significant impact on functioning of educational and research institutions. Modern educational institutions are therefore engaging themselves pro-actively in use of tools for Technology Enhanced Learning (TEL). We at SMS, Varanasi, view Information technology not only as critical infrastructure needed for supporting modern academic needs but also as a central component of the academic process itself by facilitating in various academic and professional courses. Computer Lab is equipped with latest possible Computer Application Software.

Moreover students are provided the fully air-conditioned Computer Centre with a state-of-the-art Facilities to meet the requirements of liberalized economy. The Computer Centre has one Server Room and Eight Labs having capacity of more than 400 Smart terminals with 4 GB RAM to work as Standalone and also on LAN environment, along with 6 Servers to provide Networking and backup support.

Internet : Likewise unique infrastructure, SMS is offering Wi-Fi internet facility through high speed internet via Optical Fiber.

The other saliencies include:

  • Unlimited high speed internet access via Optical Fiber.
  • Wi-Fi Enable Campus(24X7)
  • Configured and managed own Web Server.
  • Network Environment through windows 2003 and 2008 server Enterprise Edition on a HP, HCl and IBM server providing Client-Server features.
  • Multiple Operating System facility like Windows 7, Windows 10,Linux.
  • Scanner Canon LIDE 110, Canon LIDE 120
  • Multimedia computing facility.
  • Microsoft Campus Agreement.
  • Software including Compilers, RDBMS, Packages and Application software.
  • On-Line Uninterrupted Power Supply facility

Networking :

All the above systems are Networked through windows 2008/2003 Server and Linux using :

  • 24-Port 1000 Mbps Switch : D-Link
  • 24-Port Modular Patch Panel : D-Link
  • CAT – 6 Cable
  • 42 U Floor Standing Rack
  • 6 U Wall Mount Rack
  • Duplex and Simplex Information Outlet : D-Link
  • 24 PORT CISCO Managed Switch
  • D-Link Access Point for Wifi
