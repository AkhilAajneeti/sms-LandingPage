Current Year Placement 2025-26

We are swollen with pride to announce that in the first phase of students recruitment in various organizations, School of Management Sciences Varanasi invited more than fifty Marketing, Finance, HR and IT companies in its college campus especially for MBA & MCA students. College could invite companies operating in different functional areas including IT Companies, Entertainment Companies, Consumer Durable Organizations, Online Marketing, Securities, Auto Companies, Banks, FMCG Organizations, Transport Companies, Pharma Companies, Retail Giants & Infrastructure related Organizations. In this way, SMS, Varanasi manages the maximum possible placement opportunities even better better than plenty of other MBA Colleges.

Placement in a good organization is dream of most of the students, after completion of their PG program. School of Management Sciences appreciates such expectations of its future managers, therefore likewise every year, we have again arranged a scheduled and well planned Placement Season divided in two rounds. First round of our placement activities start from the month of October every year and ends by December 31st. Second Round initiates from January onwards to supplement the activities of first round. In the same fashion this year also, number of corporate have appreciated potential of student in MBA, MCA and other courses at SMS, Varanasi, & visited the institute for Campus Placement purposes.
