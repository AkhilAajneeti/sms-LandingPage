Placement Track Record

The Placement record at School of Management Sciences over the past few years has been exemplary as acknowledged by the top grade corporate houses of the country, especially in MBA Courses. SMS, Varanasi has been facilitator in inviting frontline corporate houses to its campus, for student-industry interaction and final placement of students in various corporate houses. Our students have proved their metal, as per corporate expectations..
